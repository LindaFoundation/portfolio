Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a resilient and healthy society where all individuals especially women, children, and vulnerable populations can thrive through equitable access to quality Nutrition, Healthcare, and Social Support Services.

Mission

To improve lives and promote dignity by delivering integrated, community-driven solutions in Nutrition, Health, WASH, and Protection services. We empower caregivers, support health systems, and collaborate with stakeholders to ensure sustainable development and humanitarian impact in underserved communities.

What We Do

Nutrition

In Northeast Nigeria, The Linda Foundation employs various strategies and interventions to address the Nutrition sector's challenges.

WASH

We implement sustainable WASH interventions in Northeast Nigeria to combat waterborne diseases and improve health through rehabilitation of boreholes, latrines and handwashing facilities while promoting hygiene education.

Protection

The Linda Foundation denounces all forms of human rights violations before, during and after crisis, especially those of children (child protection) and Women (GBV). Our interventions are geared towards supporting and assisting victims to become more empowered.

Health

We support programming for Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) and Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH).

Education

Through our programming, we ensure safe and inclusive education for all in all situations, including Education in Emergency (EIE).

Backyard Gardening

The Linda Foundation promotes backyard gardening to enhance household nutrition and food security in communities.

Advocacy

Through our public and private advocacy, we raise awareness of Humanitarian crisis and the needs of affected people. We advocate for the health and protection of Children, Girl child and Women and respect National and International law.

Early Recovery & Livelihoods

Strengthen access to livelihoods by creating income-generating opportunities, improving access to live skill acquisition, and basic social services, and supporting social cohesion through working for and with communities.

Recent Updates

New Nutrition Program Launched

June 15, 2023

We have launched a new nutrition program targeting 5,000 children in Borno State. The program focuses on providing nutritional supplements and education to mothers.

WASH Facilities Rehabilitation

Our team has completed the rehabilitation of 15 boreholes and construction of latrines in three communities, benefiting over 3,000 people.

Partnership with Local Government

We have entered into a partnership with the local government to expand our education in emergency programs to reach more children in conflict-affected areas.

About The Linda Foundation

The Linda Community Engagement, Nutrition, Health and Care Foundation (The Linda Foundation) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the health and well-being of vulnerable populations especially women, children, and IDPs in Nigeria.

Founded with the mission to create lasting change in underserved communities, we work across multiple sectors to deliver integrated solutions that address the root causes of poverty and vulnerability.

Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that our programs have maximum impact and create sustainable development in the communities we serve.
